The Blossom is a living space of unsurpassed comfort, designed to the highest standards. The residence located on the 2nd floor of an apartment building of the 70s consists of a comfy living room, a fully-equipped modern kitchen, a master bedroom with a private bathroom, a second bedroom that can sleep two (kids or adults), a luxurious main bathroom and a blooming balcony.

The Spaces

Master bedroom

The Blossom’s master bedroom is the embodiment of comfort and relaxation: it features a king-size bed with an anatomic mattress, a selection of CocoMat pillows, and a 55’’ Smart TV. The ensuite bathroom with a shower cabin is an extra indulgence, while the desk can serve those who combine business with leisure. Following the same homely philosophy, the second bedroom has bunk beds with anatomic mattresses and CocoMat pillows, perfectly designed for a good night’s sleep for two kids or two adults (beds’ size is 2.0m x 0.90m). The understated sophistication is also present in the main bathroom that features a bathtub with a rain shower in a glass cabin, and elegant furnishings.

Kitchen & Living room

Discrete luxury meets authentic quality at the open concept kitchen and living room of The Blossom. Equipped with high-end appliances, the kitchen offers everything you need for home cooking and dining. The light-filled living room, which connects seamlessly to the kitchen and dining area, is the perfect spot to unwind: a comfy sofa, a 65’’ Smart TV, a selection of coffee table books, and the views to the verdant balcony create a laid-back atmosphere.

Private Balcony

The private balcony is where The Blossom literally flourishes! Plants and aromatic herbs from the Mediterranean, a dreamy hanging chair, and cozy outdoor furniture compose a serene retreat. Chill and repose with a glass of wine, a cocktail, or a snack, away from the city buzz.

The Blossom is a genuine home away from home, equipped with all home-life essentials, ideal for anyone who seeks upscale hospitality in Heraklion, Crete: couples, families, business travelers, digital nomads, or friends travelling together.

House Rules

The Blossom residence is designed to offer a premium living experience. To ensure our high-quality facilities are respected and preserved, we have outlined a list of house rules that we ask our guests to follow.
Please take the time to read these house rules prior to booking with us. If you have any questions, to avoid misunderstandings, please email us at: ….
1. Please respect check-in and check-out times. Check-in is at 15:00, and check-out is at 11:00 am. For late check-out please contact us at …
2. No parties or events or third-party visitors allowed. Registered guests only.
3. No smoking allowed inside the apartment. Smoking is allowed at the balcony, and an ashtray is provided for cigarette butts. Please do not dispose of butts in the flower boxes and pots.
4. No pets allowed.
5. The Blossom Premium Living Residence is suitable for babies, toddlers, and children under 12.
6. The Blossom residence is located at an apartment building, and we want to keep our neighbours happy. Please respect the “quiet hours” as designated by the local noise ordinances: - Summer season (1 April – 30 September): 15:00-17:30 & 23:00- 07:00 - Winter season (1 October – 31 March): 15:30-17:30 & 22:00-07:30
7. Please take care of the furnishings and report any damages immediately. Any damages that exceed the deposit must be paid for by guests.
8. Please keep the space clean. Before you check out, please ensure that you place dirty dishes on the dishwasher, used towels and linen in the bathtub, lock doors and turn off all electric appliances and take the garbage out
9. Please take your shoes off when you enter the residence and place them on the rack. Slippers are provided for your convenience.
10. No illegal substances allowed on the premises.
11. Parking is not allowed at the building’s pilotis space. The open ground-floor parking area is strictly reserved for the permanent residents of the building.
