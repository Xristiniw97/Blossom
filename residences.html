Digital Nomads

The Blossom Premium Living Residence is an inspiring place to live and work. Understanding the diverse and unique needs of business travellers and digital nomads, we created an accommodation of comfort and discretion, a space that combines a homelike feeling with all the necessary amenities for working. Connectivity is essential for any corporate traveller who wants to catch up on those never ending emails and conference calls. The Blossom offers free WiFi at 50Mbps, Ethernet sockets, and USB charging sockets for your convenience. A workspace is set in the main bedroom including a desk, an office chair, and a printer (upon request). Naturally, you’re free to work and get inspired in the other spaces of the residence as well: the living room or the beautifully furnished balcony. Fully equipped with anything you might need during your stay, from making coffee to preparing a snack, or even ironing your shirts, The Blossom Premium Living Residence is the ideal place to call “home” while being on the road around the clock. The Blossom is conveniently located in the centre of Heraklion, Crete, making it easy to get around and explore. There are a lot of dining and nightlife choices close by for all travellers who want to combine business with pleasure.

Long term stays

If you are looking for a long-term stay in Heraklion, Crete, either if you are a leisure or business traveller, you’re probably expecting more than just a place to sleep. The Blossom Premium Living Residence offers more than what any impersonal hotel room can offer: space, comfort, discretion, and a priceless homey sensation. The bright and airy interiors of the residence, the fully equipped kitchen, the in-home laundry facilities, the free WiFi and entertainment options (HD TV with Netflix, board games, Bose sound system, etc.) are the things that really make a difference for an extended stay. The Blossom’s location on an apartment building in the centre of Heraklion will help you stay connected to the city and encourage you to sample life like a local.
