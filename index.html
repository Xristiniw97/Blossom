Welcome to our premium living residences

The Blossom Premium Living Residence is a flourishing haven in the heart of the historical center of Heraklion, Crete. Designed and furnished with great attention to detail. The Blossom is a living space of unsurpassed comfort, designed to the highest standards. The residence located on the 2nd floor of an apartment building of the 70s consists of a comfy living room, a fully-equipped modern kitchen, a master bedroom with a private bathroom, a second bedroom that can sleep two (kids or adults), a luxurious main bathroom and a blooming balcony.
